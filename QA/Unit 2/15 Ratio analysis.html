Question 1

[1066981] Aiko and Aimi are discussing diluted earning per shares, which of the following statements is correct?
I
Aiko - diluted EPS can be calculated as profit distributable to ordinary shares / number of ordinary shares
II
Aiko - if EPS is lower than diluted EPS then EPS should be used
III
Aimi - diluted EPS includes the effect of warrants and convertible bonds
IV
Aimi - conversion of dilutive instruments increases share volume without raising revenue, so diluted EPS is always lower than EPS
A
Both of Aiko's statements are correct and one of Aimi's statements is correct
B
One of Aiko's statements is correct and one of Aimi's statements is correct
C
Neither of Aiko's statements are correct and one of Aimi's statements is correct
D
Neither of Aiko's statements are correct and both of Aimi's statements are correct
The correct answer is: B - One of Aiko's statements is correct and one of Aimi's statements is correct

Explanation

I Aiko is incorrect: Diluted EPS can be calculated as, (profit distributable to ordinary shares + proceeds from dilutive instruments) / (number of ordinary shares + new shares created by dilutive instruments). II Aiko is correct : EPS is a conservative measure so the lowest figure should be used. III Aimi is correct : Diluted EPS includes the effect of warrants and convertible bonds. IV Aimi is incorrect : Conversion of dilutive instruments increases share volume and may raise revenue, so diluted EPS may be higher or lower than EPS.

Question 25

[1013723] Given that the current ratio = 1.2x, quick ratio = 0.8x, and current assets = £18 million, what is the value of inventories?
A
£6m
B
£8m
C
£10m
D
£12m
The correct answer is: A - £6m

Explanation

Current ratio = current assets / current liabilities. Quick ratio = (current assets - inventory) / current liabilities. First we use the current ratio to calculate the current liabilities (CL): 1.2 = 18m / CL. CL = 18m / 1.2 = 15m. Next we use this and the quick ratio to calculate the inventory (Inv). 0.8 = (18m - Inv) / 15m. 0.8 x 15m = 18m - Inv. 12m = 18m - Inv. Move the Inv to the left and the 12m to the right of the equation. Inv = 18m - 12m = 6m.
