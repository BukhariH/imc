1176

Question 11

[1013251] Which kind of index is the Nikkei 225?
A
Weighted arithmetic
B
Unweighted geometric
C
Weighted geometric
D
Unweighted arithmetic
The correct answer is: D - Unweighted arithmetic

Explanation

The Nikkei 225 is a Japanese share price index. Each share has an equal affect on the value of the index. It is unweighted. It is calculated by taking the sum of the values of the constituent shares now and dividing this by their value on the base, or beginning date; this is an arithmetic index.

Question 15

[1013249] Which of the following regarding a geometric index is/are correct?
I
If a constituent of the index falls to zero, the value of the index falls to zero
II
It would be more sensitive to large changes than a simple arithmetic index
III
Its value is always greater than, or equal to, a simple arithmetic price index on the same constituents
A
I only
B
I and II
C
II and III
D
I, II and III
The correct answer is: A - I only

Explanation

As a geometric index is calculated by multiplying constituent's price together, if one of them falls to zero, the whole index value falls to zero also. The construction of geometric averages and indices means that they always give lower values than arithmetic averages or indices; this results in the geometric index being less sensitive, and always lower in value, than an arithmetic index.
