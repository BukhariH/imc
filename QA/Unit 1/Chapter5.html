Question 3

A
Futures
B
Forward foreign exchange contracts
C
Depository receipts
D
Warrants
The correct answer is: B - Forward foreign exchange contracts

Explanation

The insider dealing legislation relates specifically to abuses of information in respect of securities markets. This therefore includes instruments based on securities, such as stock index futures, share warrants and equity options. It does not relate to FX markets, such as foreign exchange forward contracts. Additionally, the legislation does not cover assets with no secondary market, such as unit trusts.

Question 50
