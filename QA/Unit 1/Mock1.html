Question 1

[1070280] Marie Sanchez has taxable earned income of £30,000 (after taking into account her personal allowance). She realised a capital gains of £4,000 after using her CGT allowance. How much CGT is she due on this gain?
A
£880.70
B
£720
C
£933.50
D
£1,200
The correct answer is: C - £933.50

Explanation

The basic rate band ends at £31,865. Marie's gain moves beyond this band. For this reason she will be taxed on the gain partly at the basic rate (18%) and partly at the higher rate (28%). The first £1,865 of the gain is taxed at 18%: £1,865 x 0.18 = £335.70 The remaining £2,135 is taxed at 28%: £2,135 x 0.28 = £597.80 The tax due on the gain is: £335.70 + £597.80 = £933.50
